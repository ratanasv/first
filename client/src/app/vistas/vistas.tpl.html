VISTAS

Funded by the National Science Foundation (BIO/DBI 1062572) and joint among scientists at The Evergreen State College, Oregon State University, and elsewhere, develops visual analytics software to enable scientists to better understand and communicate about large and complex environmental problems that span spatial and temporal scales.
